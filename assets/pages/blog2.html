Domestic PV sales up 3.7% to 3.34 lakh units in November 2023: SIAM

12 Dec, 2023 | 2 mins read | Vashu

The total production of passenger vehicles, three- wheelers, two- wheelers, and Quadricycle in November 2023 was 22,28,743 units, according to the data released by the Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM).

Passenger Vehicles sales in the domestic market, with a growth rate of 3.7%, were at 3,34,130 units in November 2023. In the month, sales of three-wheelers were at 59,738 units, and two-wheelers at 16,23,399 units, SIAM said in a media release.

The data relating to BMW, Mercedes, JLR, Tata Motors and Volvo Auto are not available. For Tata Motors only domestic sales data is included, the SIAM release said.
